    Irietek Products

    Irietek focuses on the development of software that will afford businesses valuable tools to compete in an increasingly technical market. Our tools aim to automate tasks and provide services with minimal effort or cost to our customers. Most notable are the projects code-named "La Kaye" and "Fish Tea" respectively. More information on these two products can be found in the menu to the left.

    We are working on several other products to fill customer service, marketing and analysis requirements for small businesses. In the coming weeks, we will be unveiling some of these products. So we ask that you check back often or sign up on our product update list to be alerted when new products are released.

    In addition to these products, we sponsor several Community Projects that are open to developers and end-users who apply. As supporters of the Open Source movement, we introduce these projects as a way to illustrate the benefits of community based collaboration.
